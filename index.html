About Our Association

Our Endless Battle is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children and widows through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.

Founded in 2020, our association has helped over 500 children and 150 widows regain hope and dignity. We believe every child deserves a chance to thrive and every widow deserves support to rebuild her life.

Our team of dedicated volunteers and professionals work tirelessly to provide shelter, education, vocational training, and psychological support to those who need it most.

We operate in several regions across the country, with our main center located in the capital city. Our programs are designed to create sustainable change by addressing both immediate needs and long-term solutions.
